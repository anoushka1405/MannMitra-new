Services

At Mann Mitra, we aim to provide initial mental wellness support through a compassionate AI assistant. Here’s what we offer:

  • 🧠 AI Chat Support
    Engage in meaningful, human-like conversations with our AI assistant — designed to listen, respond empathetically, and help you process emotions.

  • 🎓 Student Support
    Academic stress, peer pressure, or exam anxiety — Mann Mitra provides a safe space for students to open up and find emotional relief.

  • 🏢 Workplace Wellness
    Help your employees maintain emotional balance and mental resilience. Mann Mitra can be integrated into corporate wellness programs to offer round-the-clock support.

  • 💬 Telehealth Companion
    Supplement existing mental health services with Mann Mitra’s accessible chatbot, especially for users needing support between therapy sessions.

  • 🔒 Privacy-First Platform
    All interactions are confidential and secured with end-to-end encryption. We follow privacy-by-default principles — no data is stored unless explicitly permitted.
