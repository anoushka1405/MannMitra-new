About

Mann Mitra is a generative AI-based chatbot designed to support mental health through empathetic conversation. Built using advanced NLP technologies like OpenAI’s GPT and Hugging Face Transformers, it offers human-like interaction to those seeking relief from stress, anxiety, or emotional overwhelm.

While it’s not a replacement for professional therapy, Mann Mitra serves as a readily accessible emotional companion, helping users feel heard and supported — anytime, anywhere.

Key Features:

  • Emotionally sensitive conversations
  • 24/7 availability
  • Private and anonymous interaction
  • Simple and clean user interface

Our Mission:

To make mental health support more approachable, stigma-free, and always available, using the power of ethical and responsible AI.

Developed by:

A team of passionate students from IGDTUW in collaboration with Sansoftech, as part of the Generative AI & Prompt Engineering Internship Program 2025.
